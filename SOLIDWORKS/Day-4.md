Step 1: Start Sketch
Open SolidWorks → New Part
Select Front Plane
Click Sketch
🔵 PART A: Create All Circles (Pulleys)
Step 2: Draw Left Small Pulley
Draw two concentric circles
Dimensions:
Outer: Ø90 mm
Inner: Ø45 mm
Step 3: Draw Center Large Pulley
Draw two concentric circles to the right
Dimensions:
Outer: Ø180 mm
Inner: Ø120 mm
Position:
Distance from left pulley center: 225 mm
Step 4: Draw Right Small Pulley
Draw two concentric circles at an angle
Dimensions:
Outer: Ø60 mm
Inner: Ø30 mm
📐 PART B: Position the Right Pulley
Step 5: Apply Angular Constraint
Draw a construction line from center of large pulley to right pulley
Apply angle:
150° (from horizontal reference)
Step 6: Apply Distance
Center distance from large pulley:
300 mm
🔗 PART C: Create Belt Connections (Tangent Lines)
Step 7: Draw Top Tangent Line (Left → Center)
Draw a line connecting:
Outer edge of left pulley
Outer edge of large pulley
Apply Tangent relation to both circles
Step 8: Draw Bottom Tangent Line (Left → Center)
Repeat for bottom side
Apply Tangent relations
Step 9: Connect Center → Right Pulley
Draw:
One top tangent line
One bottom tangent line
Apply tangency to:
Large pulley
Right pulley
✂️ Step 10: Trim Excess Geometry
Use Trim Entities
Remove unwanted arc portions inside belt region
Keep only outer visible profile
✅ Step 11: Fully Define Sketch
Ensure:
All circles are constrained
All lines are tangent
Angles & distances applied
Sketch should show: Fully Defined
🧱 Step 12: (Optional 3D) Extrude
Go to Features → Extruded Boss/Base
Apply thickness (e.g., 10–20 mm)
🔍 Summary
Model consists of:
3 pulleys (Ø90, Ø180, Ø60 outer)
Concentric inner holes
Tangent belt connections
Angular positioning (150°)
